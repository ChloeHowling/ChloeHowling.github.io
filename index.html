POEM

METAL THREAD
___
About anxiety.
___
Stressed, tightened
Thin strings shine of metal
Couldn't be harmed
Only rust can eat away the pain
The discomfort
Its existence

Nobody can see it
They might be able to sense that you have it
They might think they know how it looks
But nobody can see it
Nobody sees it

It is my secret metal thread
___

Personal Interests

I don't do much sport, but I do love running. Especially barefoot running. It was a challenge to go from running in cushioned sports shoes to running in zero-drop shoes. The sensory coming from running feets makes the exercise that much more enjoyable.

Something I recently got into doing is candle making. You can infuse color, scent, and other cool things into your candle. Just make sure don't burn down your house when experimenting with different types of wax.
